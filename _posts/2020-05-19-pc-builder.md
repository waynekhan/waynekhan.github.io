## PC Builder

Recently, I built a desktop PC for myself. My parts were shortlisted from Dreamcore Ghost V3.0, a "perfect eSports PC" according to the Singapore-based company that markets it. I considered it for some time, but ultimately decided against it as it was more expensive than I'd prefer, and I thought it'd be a fun exercise.

### Dreamcore Ghost V3.0

I learned about this company sometime in late 2019/2020, and the base price is $740 so it is indeed quite attractive, at least at first glance. To dig into the offer further, I visited [https://www.dreamcore.com.sg/product/dreamcore-ghost-v3/](https://www.dreamcore.com.sg/product/dreamcore-ghost-v3/) and customized a build. At that time (circa early May 2020), the Ryzen 3 3300X wasn't available yet, and it spurred my decision to build my own. As of today, the overall system price I arrived at was **$1725**. This I believe to be post-tax (i.e., GST), as businesses here are a bit nicer, and tend to show post-tax pricing upfront.
