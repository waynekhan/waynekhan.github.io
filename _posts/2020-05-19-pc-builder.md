## PC Builder

Recently, I built a desktop PC for myself. My parts were shortlisted from Dreamcore Ghost V3.0, a "perfect eSports PC" according to the Singapore-based company that markets it. I considered it for some time, but ultimately decided against it due to cost. (Plus I thought that it'd be a fun exercise.)

Here goes then, I visted [https://www.dreamcore.com.sg/product/dreamcore-ghost-v3/](https://www.dreamcore.com.sg/product/dreamcore-ghost-v3/) and customized the parts accordingly. The price I arrived at was **$1725** which I believe is post-tax, as businesses here tend to show post-tax pricing upfront:
