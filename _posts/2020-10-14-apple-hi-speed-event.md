## Apple Hi, Speed Event

YouTube video page: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KR0g-1hnQPA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KR0g-1hnQPA).

### HomePod Mini

![HomePod Mini]({{ site.url }}/assets/homepod-mini.png)

* Amazing sound, but I can't describe it with any technical depth.
* [S5 processor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple-designed_processors#Apple_S5), the same one used in Watch Series 5 and SE, for "computation audio"; e.g., optimize loudness, adjust dynamic range, and control the movement of its own internals.
* Multiple units can be placed throughout the house for synchronized output, or paired up when placed in the same room.
* Includes the U1 processor, so it'll know to handoff to your iPhone when it's close; e.g., show the song playing onscreen -- presumably Apple Music. [Ed: This feature comes "later this year", and it's October 14.]
* "Play all your favourite content"; e.g., iHeartRadio, radio.com, and TuneIn.
* And further down the road: Pandora, and Amazon (Music).
* Siri: Voice control of your HomeKit accessories.
* Intercom, to quickly send a (voice) message from one HomePod to another, or to multiple rooms. You know, like [an intercom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intercom). [Ed: This will also work with iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, CarPlay, and AirPods.]
* Comes in white or space grey for USD 99, start shipping mid-November.

### iPhone 12

![iPhone 12]({{ site.url }}/assets/iphone-12.png)

* [5G cellular connectivity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/5G).
* New design with flat sides, and in 5 colours (black, white, red, green, blue).
* Same 6.1" display like iPhone 11, but 11+% thinner, smaller, and lighter. [Ed: The pixel density has doubled, too.]
* "Super Retina XDR": OLED, 2,000,000:1 contrast ratio, 460 ppi.
* "Ceramic Shield", 4x better drop performance.
* "Smart Data Mode": step up/down between LTE/5G.

![A14 Bionic]({{ site.url }}/assets/a14-bionic.png)

* [A14 Bionic processor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple-designed_processors#Apple_A14_Bionic): 6-core CPU, 4-core GPU, 16-core "Neural Engine".
* Dual-camera system: ultra wide, and wide lenses.
* "Night Mode", available on all 3 cameras -- including the front.
* Video capture, it's even better than earlier iterations, check out this [video shot (and edited) on iPhone 12](https://youtu.be/KR0g-1hnQPA?t=2096).
* The return of "MagSafe", this time for iPhone. [Ed: It uses magnets to snap the device to its charging accessory, and is supposed to work with existing Qi chargers.]
* MagSafe Duo accessory, to charge iPhone and Apple Watch simultaneously. [AirPower is dead, long live AirPower!](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AirPower_(charging_mat))
* Apple will no longer ship wired Lightning headphones and adaptors, but will still include a Lightning-to-USB-C in the box.
* Starts from USD 799.

### iPhone 12 Mini

* iPhone 12 Mini, 5.4" display, and smaller/lighter than the SE.
* Starting from USD 699.

### iPhone 12 Pro

![iPhone 12 Pro]({{ site.url }}/assets/iphone-12-pro.png)

* Surgical-grade stainless steel for the bands.
* 4 colours (silver, graphite, gold, blue).
* "Ceramic Shield", 4x better drop performance.
* IP68 rating.
* 6.1" display, 0.3" larger than its predecessor and it looks taller at least.
* "Deep Fusion", on all 4 cameras -- including the front.

### iPhone 12 Pro Max

* 6.7" display, 0.2" larger than its predecessor and taller too.
* 5x optical zoom range.
* Shooting in "Apple ProRaw", available later this year. [Ed: It'll also be available for 12 Pro.]
* "Apple ProRaw" API, so third-party apps can do so too.
* Videography improved by leaps and bounds; e.g., capture/edit in Dolby Vision HDR at 4k resolution and 60 fps.
* LiDAR scanner for AR, autofocus accuracy and reduced capture time. [Ed: It might even improve Portrait shots, too.]
* Starting from USD 1099.
