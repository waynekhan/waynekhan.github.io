## Maximizing GETGREAT STARS for KrisFlyer miles

[GETGREAT](https://www.greateasternlife.com/sg/en/mobile-apps/getgreat.html) is a platform to earn STARS (stars). I use it on iOS, and as the new month is coming up, I created a few tables. In my case, I'm on iOS and using the app in Singapore, and 1,000 stars translates redeemed to 330 KrisFlyer miles, so we can min/max this once we have a good understanding of the system. There is also a caveat that I'll get into later on.

Also, I hope Singapore Airlines doesn't go out of business. But I digress. Back to GETGREAT. Now there are at least 3 features that are useful:

### 1. FitAge rewards

FitAge can range from +3 (bad) to -3 (good) years relative to your actual age, and stars here are earned on a daily basis:

|          |-1 |-2 |-3 |
|----------|---|---|---|
|Stars     |1  | 2 | 3 |

### 2. Daily Great Walkathon rewards

Walking from 6k (good) to 15k (best) steps earns stars. At this point, I'd like to introduce XP (experience), which in sufficient quantities (i.e., "level up") also earns stars. Experience and stars here are earned on a daily basis:

|          |6k |8k |10k|15k|
|----------|---|---|---|---|
|Experience|60 |110|140|160|
|Stars     |8  |15 |20 |22 |

### 3. 7-Day Program rewards

7-Day Programs are rated in terms of sweat droplets, which I'll term as intensity (1 to 3). Experience here is earned on a _weekly_ basis:

|          | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|----------|---|---|---|
|Experience|200|270|345|

For May 2020, we can earn between 496 to 775 stars, depending on participation:

|          |-1 FitAge/8k steps/1 intensity |
|----------|-------------------------------|
|Experience|2,940                          |
|Stars     |496                            |

|          |-3 FitAge/15k steps/3 intensity|
|----------|-------------------------------|
|Experience|6,340                          |
|Stars     |775                            |

The shortfall (4 and 225) can be overcome as leveling up earns stars. A caveat, though: __stars expire at the end of each month.__, and can only be redeemed 500 (or 1000) at once.

Some worked examples should clarify any outstanding doubts:

* At levels 101+, leveling up thrice (3000 experience) is sufficent; i.e., 775+(76*3) = __1,003 stars__.
* At between levels 92 through 100, leveling up 4 times (4000 experience) is sufficent; i.e., 775+(66*4) = __1,039 stars__.
* At between levels 82 through 91, leveling up 4 times (3600 experience) is sufficent; i.e.,  775+(60*4) = __1,015 stars__.

And of course, any outstanding, unredeemed stars are forfeit come June, July, and so on and so forth.
